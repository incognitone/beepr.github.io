5 Reasons why our app is different than any other social media app!

1. You will keep your full anonimity to yourself! So no need for telephone numbers, Facebook profiles or other anonimity comprimising information.

2. Our app uses sound to connect two mobile devices with each other contrary to other apps that use bluetooth or other wireless technologies.

3. The first time you want to connect with another individual using beepr has to be a physical social interaction in order to transfer/receive the sound beepr emits, so you will have seen your chat partner in real life!

4. Our app will give everybody a random name and picture once you have connected with eachother, not happy with the name? You can change it right away!

5. No difficult settings menu's, unnecessary elaborate menu options, simple and straightforward!
