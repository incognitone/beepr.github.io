So here is a short Q&A session which also elaborates our philosophy behind the application.

Beepr


How many Facebook friends does everyone have? How many of these friends have you actually seen or spoken? Could be never, or perhaps just once. In the meantime, these 'friends' will know a lot about you, as far as you allow personal information on your profile.


The Beepr app anticipates on this situation, by starting a conversation with a person, without having to be stuck to this person, you could delete and stop the chat at any given time. Where Facebook does give you personal information on a person, the Beepr app will not provide any information, no number nor name nor location.

This file will document any questions that can be asked or are already asked by fellow students regarding Beepr.

What differentiates Beepr from other chat apps and why is it more useful?

Of course, there are a lot of other chat apps, which are very popular, simple and effective. So why would we even design another chat app, the idea isn't that original. The thing that differentiates Beepr compared to other chat apps is the fact that you will gain complete anonymity, there are no strings attached. Your contacts will not have any name, you will not need a number, nor any Facebook data. The only requirement, is matching two devices by using a sound signal. This signal includes a key which will have to authenticate with another device, which will result in a conversation between the hosts, while maintaining your anonymity, so no personal information whatsoever.

Why don't we use Bluetooth to connect two devices?

- Trustability, since the connection is acquired by sending a sound.
- No possibility of connection interference, we have complete control over communication.
- It is much faster, there will be no delay for establishing a connection between two devices (Searching for devices, request for connection)

Why the use of sound?

We wanted to come up with something original and the most of all we wanted to create a way of establishing a connection without having to provide any personal information. Also to make sure there are no miscommunications, such as providing the wrong phone number.
By using the sound you will have a guarantee that the chat session will be created and the best part of it, providing no personal data. You are able to delete the chat and never hear from the person in question again.

Isn't there already a similar chatapp?

We are all familiar with the alternative chatapps, that work with contacts and phone numbers, such as Whatsapp and Telegram. Beepr is actually something completely different, since we use a sound signal for creating a conversation and an app using this mechanism doesn't exist.

We did get inspired by an app named 'Chirp', this app uses a sound signal to transfer an image from one to device to the other. This mechanism is very much alike Beepr's mechanism, but we implemented a chat system.

How will you able to distinguish several contacts if you don't have any information about them?

Because you are not able to create your own username, it might be difficult to distinguish several contacts in your contact list, when you quickly want to contact someone. To solve this problem, we designed a Random Nickname-Generator for this app, where every contact will receive a random username from the server, which will appear on the screen. This username consists of 2 random words, first being an adjective and the second being a name.

We also included a Random Avatar-Generator in the app, which makes sure every contact will not only have a random username, but also a random 'profile picture'. These random nicknames and avatars are combined with the feature that the most recent or most used chats will appear at the top of your contact list, to easily keep track of contacts, while still maintaining your anonymity.

How is a conversation anonymous, when in order to create one, you will require a physical interaction with a certain person?

The keyword in Beepr is anonymity. But then again, it is not anonymity in the fullest meaning of the word. You could see Beepr as the contrary of the Internet, where you might have never seen or spoken a certain person, but still you could have a lot of personal data on that person. Our anonymity can be seen as some kind of safety, you will be untraceable for your contacts. 'No strings attached' and no providing of information.

Benefits of anonymity:

No providing of personal data, e.g. phone numbers, names, usernames. Also when you're chatting with a person, this person will not be able to distribute your data to his/her friend, because the person in question will have no information about you -> Anonymous. You will not be stuck to anyone, as soon as you decide to stop a conversation with a person, you can simply delete the chat and all evidence of existence will be wiped out. You will not be able to contact this person again, since you do not have anything on them. The only way you will be able to start a conversation with this person again is by matching with him again, when meeting the person in real life.

The biggest advantage about the anonymity is security. You can feel absolutely secure when using this app because we have the complete anonymity principle.

The sound responds to very specific frequencies. These frequencies are chosen to be higher than the frequency which we talk with, so even in a room with a lot of background noise or white noise, the app will still be able to recognize the sounds and establish a chat. This also gives advantages for any kind of miscommunication, when you want to make use of an app that does use personal information. Providing the wrong number or the wrong name or not understanding it very well, is no problem when using Beepr and it's sound mechanism.
